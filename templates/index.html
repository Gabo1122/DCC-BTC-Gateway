DecentralChain BTC Gateway

This is a gateway for transferring Bitcoin from and to DecentralChain.





To Send {{chainName}} to DCC,

- Fill in the receiving DCC wallet address below
- Click "Establish Tunnel"
- From your {{chainName}} wallet send the {{chainName}} tokens to the generated address
- Tunnels are one time use, they are deleted after a succesfull transfer
- If you want to verify a tunnel fill in your DCC wallet address and click "Check BTC Tunnel"

To withdraw {{chainName}} from the DCC using DCC wallet

- Click "SEND"
- Recipient's Address = {{ tnAddress }}
- Attachment = only insert your {{chainName}} wallet address
